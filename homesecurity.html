Home security with smart technology

Outdoor Camera

The Outdoor Camera Pro, with its computer vision chip, provides advanced Smart Deter for monitoring specific zones. Upon detecting prolonged presence, it sounds a loud alarm and lights up its LED ring, alerting the intruder. Your home is protected by an outdoor camera that responds autonomously.

For reliable smart home security, a strong wireless connection is essential. The Secure Home Pro Outdoor Camera Pro uses hybrid wired Wi-Fi to provide smooth, fast video, minimizing delay and buffering unlike other wireless cameras.

Indoor Camera

The leading indoor camera utilizes artificial intelligence to identify people in specific zones. Easily protect restricted areas in your home, such as a safe or gun cabinet, by setting a detection zone and getting alerts when someone nears. The Indoor Camera Pro’s smart technology ensures you get only meaningful notifications.

Featuring improved night vision, a wide field of view, and top-notch image quality, the Indoor Camera Pro ensures a clear picture both day and night. Its sleek design and adaptable mounting options make it easy to set up the camera anywhere in your home to get the view you need. Elevate your visibility and security to the next level.

Doorbell Camera

The Doorbell Camera employs Smart Deter enhanced by computer vision chip technology to accurately detect deliveries and loiterers. Upon spotting someone trying to steal a package, the camera activates a bright LED ring and speaker, warning the potential thief that they are being recorded.

The Doorbell Camera Pro features customizable surveillance zones, enabling it to cover your entire porch or just a designated area, minimizing false alerts from pets and pedestrians.
